Description:

Wildhacks is an event that is for attendees (technical & non-technical) to create an application of their choice in the time allowed. At the end, the applications will be presented then judged based on: Ability to articulate the use of your app, Idea originality, Technical Difficulty of the implementation. It is a great opportunity to network with many motivated people.

What to bring:

Your laptop, skills & ideas. Come with a collaborative, team focused mindset and/or team up in advance. Whether you are a backend person, designer, entrepreneur, student, or just interested in tech; you are invited to attend this event.
